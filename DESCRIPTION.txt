App Title: Coffee Shop – Brew & Bites

Subtitle:
A cozy cafe menu with customizable drinks and pastries.

Long description (App Store style):

Discover your new favorite cup with Coffee Shop – Brew & Bites. Browse a curated menu of handcrafted coffees, teas, and freshly baked pastries, then customize each item to match your taste. Whether you like a strong espresso, a creamy latte, or a sweet pastry, Coffee Shop makes it easy to build the perfect order.

Features:
- Browse high-quality images of drinks and pastries.
- Customize drink sizes, milk types, and add-ons (syrups, shots, extras).
- Add items to your cart and adjust quantities before checkout.
- Search and filter popular items for quick ordering.
- Delightful confirmation animations (confetti) after placing an order.

Perfect for cafe owners, baristas, and coffee lovers, Coffee Shop showcases a modern SwiftUI UI with simple state management and a focus on delightful, pixel-perfect visuals.
